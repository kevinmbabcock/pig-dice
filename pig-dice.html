Welcome to the Pig Dice Game

Here are the rules to the game:

  • Roll a standard dice with numbers 1 through 6
  • If the rolled number is 1, that players turn is over and they pass the die to the next player without recording any score
  • If any other number is rolled, that number is added to their current score, and they have the option to roll again or hold, which will add their current score (for that round) to their total score for the game.
  • First player to score 100 points wins
